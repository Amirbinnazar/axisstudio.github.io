About Us We serve, protect and install beautiful monuments

Act 12 of 2018.- Sri Nadaprabhu Kempegowda was the founder and developer of the city of Bengaluru and several other Heritage sites and Monuments. Therefore, it is considered necessary to establish an Authority for development and maintenance of the heritage sites, tourist places and monuments of Nadaprabhu Kempegowda, in and around the Bengaluru Urban, Bengaluru Rural, Chikkaballapura, Ramanagara and Tumkuru Districts of Bengaluru Revenue Division into International heritage cultural and Tourist center. Hence, the Bill. [L.A. Bill No.63 of 2018 File No. Samvyashae 51 Shasana 2017] [entry 32 of List II and 40 of List III of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India.] KARNATAKA ACT NO. 12 OF 2018 (First Published in the Karnataka Gazette Extra-ordinary on the 17th day of March, 2018) THE NADAPRABHU KEMPEGOWDA HERITAGE AREA DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY ACT, 2018 (Received the assent of Governor on the 12th day of March, 2018)
