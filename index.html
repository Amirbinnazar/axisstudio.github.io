About Us We serve, protect and install beautiful monuments

An Act to provide for the establishment of an Authority to take up conservation and development of Kempegowda heritage area, tourism places and monuments and to implement the programmes, considering the efforts of Nadaprabhu Kempegowda for the development of Bengaluru.

Whereas, it is expedient to provide for the establishment of an Authority for development and maintenance of the heritage area, tourism places and monuments of Nadaprabhu Kempegowda in and around Bengaluru Urban, Bengaluru Rural, Chikkaballapura, Ramanagara and Tumkuru District of Bengaluru Revenue Division into international heritage, cultural and tourist center.
